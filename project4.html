Smart Fire Detection Alarm

Introduction

The Smart Fire Detection Alarm is an IoT-based system that detects fire and gas leaks, providing real-time alerts and triggering alarms to prevent hazards.

Working Principle

The system monitors the environment with flame and gas sensors, triggering a buzzer and sending remote alerts to the IoT cloud if a fire or excessive gas is detected. This ensures early detection and rapid response to fire hazards.

Components Used

  • ESP8266 (NodeMCU)
  • Flame Sensor
  • MQ-4 Gas Sensor
  • Buzzer

Applications

  • Home Fire Safety Monitoring
  • Industrial Hazard Prevention
  • Warehouse Safety Systems
